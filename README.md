## 🔍 How to Use the API

Open the application after the setup process finishes. The main dashboard displays your current app track list. You can add a new mobile app by typing the app store URL into the search field. The software will pull data regarding your current rank, estimated keyword traffic, and competitor movement.

Select the Keywords tab to perform your first search. Type a general term related to your app, such as "fitness tracker" or "photo editor." The system will return a list of related keywords. Look at the difficulty score to see how hard it is to rank for a specific phrase. A lower score typically means less competition.

## 📊 Understanding Store Charts

The Charts section provides a visual history of your app rankings over time. You will see line graphs representing your position in the store. An upward trend suggests your recent updates or marketing efforts show positive results. A downward trend indicates that you may need to adjust your keyword strategy.

You can compare two apps side-by-side. Select one app from your list and drag it into the comparison slot. Then, select a competitor app. The system generates a table showing side-by-side keyword performance.

## 💡 Best Practices for Keyword Research

* Research at least twenty keywords every month.
* Monitor your top three competitors to stay ahead.
* Focus on long-tail keywords which are phrases with three or more words.
* Update your app title and description based on the high-traffic terms you identify.
* Test new keywords by monitoring your rank change for two weeks.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

If the application fails to fetch data, check your internet status. Restart the application if the dashboard remains blank for more than ten seconds. If you see an error message, copy the text and verify your firewall settings. Sometimes, corporate networks block data traffic to app store servers. If you use a virtual private network, try turning it off to see if the connection improves. You can uninstall and reinstall the tool if you continue to face problems. Always keep your Windows system updated to avoid compatibility issues.
